How To Play



Getting Started



Click on Start game.


Once the game is loaded, you will have 25 people listed on the screen.


To eliminate possible people from the listed inventory, you will need to ask some questions. All possible questions are located below the people.


To find out more details about an individual, you can click on them, and a detailed card will appear on the left side.


To make your guess, click the button located at the bottom of the individual's detail card. Careful though! You only get two guesses!

Game Description



This is a company-focused guessing game designed to help you learn more about your coworkers.


It was designed to help speed up the process of meeting the new people in your life.


You'll need to figure out who the hidden person is to win.


Have fun!
