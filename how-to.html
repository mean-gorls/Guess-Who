Game Description

This game is company guessing game designed to make you learn more about your coworkers. This game was designed to help people in the company to get to know eachother better.



The GOAL of the game.

You need to figure out who the hidden person is to win. You will need to figure out who the computer has in order to win.



Getting Started

Click on “Start game.” Once the game is loaded you will have 25 of the people listed on the screen. To eliminate possible people from the listed inventory you will need to ask some questions that is located below the inventory. To find out more details about an individual you can click on the person and a detailed card will populate on the left side. To call on a person you can click the button located at the bottom of the persons details “Guess name”
